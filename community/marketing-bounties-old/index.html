      MARKETING PROGRAM

      Marketing Bounty Program

      The NEM team actively encourages the community to be involved with NEM marketing.
      We welcome and reward good writers and artists for our bounty program.

      Check our development bounties
      Development bounties

      NEM 101 Bounty

      This bounty is provided to increase general awareness of the NEM blockchain. If you are able to organize a NEM101 talk in your city, you may receive the following:

      • Up to $200 in XEMs to cover food, beverage and venue (receipts are required to claim and you are encouraged to raise external sponsorship).
      • $200in XEMs to be shared between the organizer/s of event
      • $100in XEMs for the speaker presenting NEM101 (organizer/s and speaker can be the same person)

      Bounties are available on the following terms and conditions:

      1. Organizer to organize events via Meetup platform
      2. Maximum of one (1) NEM101 per city per month.
      3. Materials on how to organize the event and present NEM101 will be sent to the organizer/s to coordinate with the speaker.
      4. Send event proposal with date, time and location to [email protected]. Event must be
        within 30 days of proposal. Not all proposal will be accepted. It will depend
        on how active you are in the NEM community, whether another organizer is
        hosting something already and other factors.
      5. The talk must be attended by a minimum of twenty (20) people.
      6. The bounty will be paid once a report is submitted with proof of attendance in the form of a signed attendance list and a group photo.
      7. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

        8. Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Published Article Bounty

      This bounty is provided to increase the exposure of the NEM blockchain in the media. The following bounties are available for:
      • $150 for self published articles.
      • $250 for articles published in mainstream media.
      Bounties are available on the following terms and conditions:
      1. The bounty panel will determine whether the article is of sufficient quality to qualify for this bounty. Author may submit the article for assessment prior to publishing to confirm if it qualifies for this bounty.
      2. Mainstream media refers to any online or print media of at least a national level
      3. The bounty will be paid once the article has been published.
      4. Only 1 bounty is allocated per media platform.
      5. The decision of the bounty panel is final.
      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Video Bounty

      This bounty is provided to those who have published a video promoting NEM. The following bounties are available for:

      • $50for a basic video
      • Up to $250 for a well-produced high-quality video.

      Bounties are available on the following terms and conditions:

      1. The bounty panel will determine the quality of the video to assign the value of the bounty.
      2. The bounty will be paid once the video has received a minimum of 500 views.
      3. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      NEM Blog Post Bounty

      This bounty is provided to those who write blog posts for the NEM blog. A bounty of $100 to $1,000 in XEMs will be paid for each published blog post.

      Bounties are available on the following terms and conditions:

      1. The blog post should be written in Markdown, which is the language used to write posts in the NEM forum and is easy to use. Dillinger.io is a very good Markdown preview site. Please make sure your pics/infographics have proper hosting links on a third-party hosting site. A writing guide can be found here.
      2. The blog post should be pasted in a Google Doc and the link submitted for assessment.
      3. Blogs can be written like articles that discuss a topic or “how to” tutorials. An example post can be found here.
      4. The bounty will be paid once the blog post has been published.
      5. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Business Community Outreach Bounty

      This bounty is provided to increase awareness of the NEM blockchain among the business community. If you are able to organize a NEM talk with a major business organization, association or chamber of commerce in your city.

      • Rewards: $100

      Bounties are available for the first 50 persons on the following terms and conditions:

      1. Your city must be one of the 10 largest cities in your country. An exception can be made if you are able to provide reasons why your city has significance.
      2. The business organization, association or chamber of commerce must be a national or state-wide body with a minimum of 500 members.
      3. The talk must be for a minimum of 45 minutes and must be conducted by a person approved by the NEM foundation.
      4. The talk must be attended by a minimum of 30 persons.
      5. The bounty will be paid once a report is submitted with proof of attendance in the form of a signed attendance list and a group photo.
      6. Only 1 bounty is allocated per city and a maximum of 5 bounties are allocated per country.
      7. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      In order to qualify for this bounty, kindly obtain pre-approval before proceeding by filling out the Pre-Approval Form. If you proceed without obtaining approval, we will not be held responsible if you fail to qualify for the bounty.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty.

      Exchange Listing Bounty

      This bounty is provided to increase the number of exchanges on which NEM is traded on. If you are able to convince an exchange to add NEM onto their trading platform, you will receive a $500 USD bounty paid in XEMs.

      Bounties are available for the first 10 persons on the following terms and conditions:

      1. The exchange must be in operation for at least 6 months.
      2. The exchange must have an overall minimum of USD $1 million traded a day.
      3. A press release must be issued announcing the addition of NEM to the exchange.
      4. The bounty will be paid once proof of the addition of NEM to the exchange is provided.
      5. Only 1 bounty is allocated per country (based on where the exchange is situated).
      6. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Global Media Feature Bounty

      This bounty is provided to increase the exposure of the NEM blockchain in the international media. If you are able to arrange for one of the NEM foundation members to be featured or interviewed on a global media platform, you will receive the following bounties:

      • Print media: $200USD bounty paid in XEMs
      • Online media: $300USD bounty paid in XEMs
      • Television media: $500USD bounty paid in XEMs

      10 bounties are available for each of the above media channels on the following terms and conditions:

      1. The media platform must have a global reach. An example of print media is TIME magazine. An example of online media is WIRED magazine. An example of television media is CNN.
      2. The bounty will be paid once the media article has been published or the program aired.
      3. Only 1 bounty is allocated per media platform.
      4. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      ICO Bounty

      This bounty is provided to encourage the use of NEM mosaics to create tokens for ICOs. If you are able to convince an ICO to adopt the NEM platform for the issuance of their tokens, you will receive a $1,000USD bounty paid in XEMs.

      Bounties are available for the first 10 persons on the following terms and conditions:

      1. The tokens created for the ICO must exclusively be a NEM mosaic.
      2. The ICO must accept XEMs from contributors.
      3. A link to NEM must be placed on the ICO website.
      4. NEM must be referenced in the whitepaper.
      5. The bounty will be paid once the ICO has been launched.
      6. Only 1 bounty is allocated per person.
      7. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Academia Bounty

      This bounty is provided to encourage serious academic research into the NEM blockchain. An academic researcher who conducts research, writes and publishes an academic paper featuring the NEM blockchain will receive a $5,000USD bounty paid in XEMs.

      Bounties are available for the first 4 persons on the following terms and conditions:

      1. The researcher must have a PhD qualification from an accredited university.
      2. The research project must be approved and endorsed by an accredited university.
      3. The academic paper must be published in an accredited journal.
      4. $1,000USD bounty will be paid once the following have been provided:
        • The research proposal
        • Proof of researcher’s qualifications
        • Proof of university’s accreditation
      5. The remaining $4,000USD will be paid once the academic paper has been published and the following have been provided:
        • A copy of the journal carrying the published paper
        • Proof of the journal’s accreditation
      6. Only 1 bounty is allocated per academic institution.
      7. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Go To Market Bounty

      This bounty is provided to support the commercialization of real-world use cases built on the NEM blockchain. If you have successfully launched a NEM-powered application and have begun to generate revenue, you will receive a $10,000USD bounty paid in XEMs.

      5 bounties are available on the following terms and conditions:

      1. This bounty is paid on a revenue-matching basis. The full bounty is paid when proof of $10,000USD revenue from the application is provided.
      2. Only 1 bounty is allocated per application.
      3. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim

      Government Project Bounty

      This bounty is provided to encourage governments and public bodies to adopt the NEM blockchain. If you are able to secure a contract with a government or a public body to build a solution using the NEM blockchain, you will receive a $5,000USD bounty paid in XEMs.

      Bounties are available for the first 10 persons on the following terms and conditions.

      1. The contract must be with a national or state government or a national or state government body.
      2. The contract value must be worth a minimum of $50,000USD.
      3. The engagement must be reported in at least one major media platform in that country.
      4. The bounty will be paid once the following has been provided:

        • A copy of the contract
        • Proof of receipt of payment of at least 10% of the contract value
        • Proof that work has commenced to fulfill the contract
      5. Only 1 bounty is allocated per country.
      6. The decision of the bounty panel is final.

      Click HERE to submit your bounty claim
